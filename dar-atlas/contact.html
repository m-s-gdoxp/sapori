FAQ

Can airport transfer be arranged?

Yes. Share flight number and arrival time. The team can arrange driver pickup and porter support for the final Medina approach.

Can dinner, spa, and guides be booked before arrival?

Yes. Send preferred dates and the concierge will prepare a proposed schedule.

Is the riad available for private buyout?

Yes, subject to dates. Buyouts are best for weddings, retreats, family celebrations, and brand shoots.

What language support is available?

The service team can support English, French, Arabic, and common traveler needs by request.
