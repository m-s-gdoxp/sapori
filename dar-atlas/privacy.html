Privacy policy

How inquiry and reservation information is handled on this hotel website template.

Information we collect

When you send an inquiry, the site may ask for your name, email, phone number, dates, guest count, and message so the hotel can respond to your request.

How information is used

Information is used only to answer reservation, dining, spa, event, or concierge inquiries and to prepare relevant hotel services.

Sharing

Information is not sold. It may be shared with trusted service partners only when needed to arrange transfers, guides, events, spa treatments, dining, or guest services requested by you.

Cookies

This static template does not require tracking cookies. If analytics, ads, booking engines, or embedded maps are added later, the hotel should update this policy and cookie notice.
